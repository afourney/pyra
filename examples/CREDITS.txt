CREDITS
=========

The file 'shakespeare.xml.gz' contains the complete works of
Shakespeare, encoded as one large XML document (roughly 7Mb).

Cetainly, the works of Shakespeare have long been in the 
public domain. This version, with XML markup, was prepared
by Jon Bosak, and has also been placed in the public domain.
